# User Stories (Refined & Consolidated)

## 1. Category & Preference Filtering
- As a user, I want to filter restaurants by food category (e.g., seafood, Chinese, dessert), so that I can quickly find places that match what I feel like eating.

---

## 2. Integrated & Trustworthy Information
- As a user, I want to see both official food safety ratings and user reviews in one place, so that I can make more informed and balanced decisions.  

- As a health-conscious diner, I want to see official safety ratings with inspection dates and links to government reports, so that I can trust the data and confidently avoid high-risk venues.

---

## 3. Safety Awareness & Control
- As a cautious diner, I want to set a minimum food safety threshold, so that I can automatically filter out restaurants that do not meet my standards.  

- As a user, I want to understand what each food safety rating means, so that I can correctly interpret and trust the information.

---

## 4. Route-Based Planning (Core Feature)
- As a traveler with a planned itinerary, I want to find restaurants along my route, so that I can fit meals into my journey without unnecessary detours.  

- As a proactive planner, I want to manually create and adjust my route with real-time updates (e.g., distance, time), so that I can maintain control over my travel plan.

---

## 5. Efficient Decision-Making
- As a user, I want to quickly find suitable restaurants through a clear and simple interface, so that I don’t spend too much time searching.  

- As a practical decision-maker, I want to see key information (e.g., opening hours, price, contact details), so that I can determine if a restaurant fits my schedule and budget.

---

## 6. Social & Trust Signals
- As a user, I want to see warnings or highlights based on other users’ feedback, so that I can avoid risky or low-quality restaurants.

---

## 7. Saving & Planning Ahead
- As a student traveler, I want to save restaurants to a wishlist and access details like availability and menus, so that I can move from discovery to final decision more easily.

---

## Summary
These user stories highlight the importance of simple, goal-driven interactions, combining trusted data, route-based exploration, and clear filtering to support efficient dining decisions in travel contexts.
