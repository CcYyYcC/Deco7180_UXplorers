# Interview Transcript

## Participant 2

**Interviewer:** Thank you for participating in our interview. During the interview, you can ask me to pause, skip questions, or delete this recording at any time. Do you allow us to record this interview?

**Participant:** Yes.

**Interviewer:** Thank you. This is the low-fidelity prototype we created. First question: if this platform were fully developed, would you use the **food safety filter** when looking for a restaurant?

**Participant:** Yes, I would actively use it when looking for a restaurant.

**Interviewer:** Why is this important to you?

**Participant:** Because food safety is the most basic standard for dining. Especially when I eat out, take my family out, or visit an unfamiliar restaurant, this function can help me quickly avoid potential health risks. It is very important to me.

**Interviewer:** Does the concept of **looking for restaurants along a planned route** match your actual travel habits?

**Participant:** Yes. Based on my travel habits, I usually move along a fixed route.

**Interviewer:** Can you give an example of when you would prefer this instead of just searching for restaurants nearby?

**Participant:** For example, when I am travelling from a hotel to a bus stop, I would look for restaurants along that planned route. This is more efficient than simply searching for nearby restaurants.

**Interviewer:** If a restaurant already has good user reviews, would the added **official food safety rating** change your opinion?

**Participant:** Yes, it would.

**Interviewer:** Why?

**Participant:** Because taste is subjective, but food safety is an objective standard.

**Interviewer:** How low would the food safety rating have to be for you to cancel a booking?

**Participant:** Generally, if the rating is below **3 stars**, I would cancel the booking directly. Health is more important than taste.

**Interviewer:** Would you trust a platform that combines **official government hygiene data** with normal restaurant search functions?

**Participant:** Yes, I would be willing to trust such a platform, because government inspection data is more authoritative than ordinary user comments.

**Interviewer:** What would the platform need to show to prove the data is authentic?

**Participant:** It should show the public data source, the inspection time, and the inspection items. Ideally, it should also provide a link to the official report, so I can be sure the information is reliable.

**Interviewer:** Based on the basic wireframes you just viewed, do you think the steps from **searching, filtering, and route planning** are logical? Is anything missing in the flow?

**Participant:** The whole process feels logical and smooth.

**Interviewer:** Is there anything you would like to add?

**Participant:** If I could add something, I would like to see a **save/favourite function**, **average cost per person**, **actual queueing situation**, and **real user reviews**. That would make the experience more complete.

**Interviewer:** Compared with the way you currently search for restaurants, such as using Google Maps, what do you think is the biggest potential advantage or disadvantage of using this kind of **safe food routing map**?

**Participant:** The biggest advantage is that it solves the food safety issue directly and in a very targeted way. The disadvantage is that, at the beginning, the number of restaurants and the richness of information may not be as strong as a mature platform like Google Maps.

**Interviewer:** Imagine the platform recommends a restaurant on your route. It has a **5-star taste rating** from users, but only a **2-star hygiene rating** from the government. How would you make your final decision?

**Participant:** I would not choose that 2-star hygiene restaurant. Hygiene and safety cannot be compromised. I would not risk my health.

**Interviewer:** Since this is only a basic draft, if you really wanted to book a table through this platform, what two key pieces of information **must** be included in the final version?

**Participant:** First, it must include an up-to-date **food safety rating** and detailed **inspection results**. Second, it must include accurate **booking rules**, **average cost per person**, **business hours**, and **contact information**. Only with this information would I feel comfortable using the platform.

**Interviewer:** Thank you for participating in our interview. I will stop the recording now.

**Participant:** Okay.
