## Interview Questions

### Open-ended Questions

**Q1 — Tourist Perspective**  
Imagine you are a first-time visitor to this city. When using this prototype, what information do you pay the most attention to? Is there anything you feel is missing or not very useful?

**Q2 — Weight Adjustment Feature**  
For the feature that allows you to adjust the weight of rating, number of reviews, and safety score, in what situations do you find it valuable? When might you choose not to use it?

**Q3 — Custom Route Feature**  
If you could set a route and see recommended restaurants along the way, in what situations would you use this feature? How might it support or limit your travel or dining decisions?

**Q4 — Interface Logic**  
While browsing the map, filtering restaurants, and adjusting features, was there any step that felt unclear or disconnected? Could you describe it?
